How to Apply

Interested in joining Agbor Technical College? Admission into the school is open to students who wish to gain practical technical and vocational skills. Below is a simple guide to help you begin your application.For more details on the application process, visit the school office or the Delta State Ministry of Technical Education for the most updated information.

01

Obtain Application Form

Visit the school to pick up the admission or registration form.

02

Submit Required Documents

Fill out the form and attach the necessary credentials before submitting.

03

Participate in Required Assessment

Applicants may go through a screening, interview, or placement process depending on the program.

04

Finalize Your Registration

Once you are offered admission, complete all registration and enrollment procedures at the school.

05

Payment of Fees
