About Agbor Technical College

Where Skills Meet Opportunity

Located in the heart of Ika South Local Government Area, Delta State, Agbor Technical College has been equipping students with industry-ready technical skills for years. Our modern workshops, experienced instructors, and hands-on approach to learning ensure that graduates are prepared to excel in construction, electrical, plumbing, catering, and other high-demand trades.

Why Choose Us

Quality Technical Training for Real-World Success

Hands-On Learning

Our modern workshops and equipment provide students with practical, hands-on experience in their chosen trade. You'll work with the same tools and materials used in professional construction sites and technical installations.

Experienced Instructors

Learn from certified professionals with years of industry experience. Our instructors bring real-world expertise to the classroom, ensuring you master the skills that employers are looking for.

Industry Certifications

Graduate with recognized certifications that open doors to employment opportunities across Delta State and Nigeria. Our programs are designed to meet industry standards and employer requirements.

Job Placement Support

We connect our graduates with employers across Delta State and beyond. Many of our students secure employment before completing their training, thanks to our strong relationships with construction companies, contractors, hotels, and technical service providers in the region.

Expert Instructors
